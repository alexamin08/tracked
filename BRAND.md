# Tracked — Brand Voice & Design Taste

## Design direction: Azure Curator (Coastal Premium)

High-end beach club at golden hour × reality TV production polish.

Font: Plus Jakarta Sans (single family, weights 300-800)
Primary: #0058bb (vibrant azure)
All pill-shaped (border-radius: 9999px) — buttons, inputs, badges, player
Glassmorphic floating elements (surface 80% opacity + backdrop-blur)
Cinematic show imagery with dark gradient thumbnails
No borders — boundaries via tonal background shifts only
Hollywood metadata: show/network callouts in uppercase labels

## Voice

Authoritative: speak with expert confidence. Value the creator's time.
Clear & precise: "5 tracks matched" not "We found some amazing options!"
Professional grace: never cute, never exclamatory, no emoji in UI copy.
"Fully licensed" not "royalty-free." Always mention Content ID protection.

## Anti-patterns

No "Welcome back!" or "Hey there!" greetings
No exclamation marks in UI labels
No stock photo aesthetic — everything cinematic and editorial
No generic "Browse our library" — it's "Describe your scene"
Never say "royalty-free music library" — say "AI music supervisor" or "broadcast-proven catalog"
