Frequently Asked

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes. Our commitment to creators means flexibility. You can cancel at any time from your account settings. Your license remains valid for any music generated during your active subscription.

Who owns the AI-generated compositions?

Tracked operates on a joint-ownership model. You own the creative performance rights for the specific rendered track, while the underlying AI model architecture remains proprietary.

Does the social license cover YouTube ads?

The Starter plan covers organic social content. For paid advertising, broadcast, or theatrical releases, our Pro or Team licenses are required to ensure full legal protection.

Are the AI tracks truly royalty-free?

Absolutely. Once you've licensed a track, you never have to pay another cent in royalties, regardless of how many views your project garners or where it is distributed.
