Technical Documentation (FAQ)

01. Licensing Scope

All tracks licensed under our platform are perpetually cleared for the medium defined in your tier. Even if you cancel, your existing content remains protected.

02. Stem Extraction

Professional and Enterprise tiers allow for 4-track stem extraction (Drums, Bass, Instruments, Vocals) using our proprietary AI isolation hardware.

03. Performance Royalties

We operate on a 100% royalty-free model. You never have to pay secondary fees to PROs (ASCAP, BMI, PRS) for digital distribution.

04. Custom Composition

Enterprise clients can request custom scores generated by our neural network and polished by human engineers within 48 hours.
