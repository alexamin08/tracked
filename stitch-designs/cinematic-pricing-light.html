Questions

How does the licensing work for YouTube?

add
Our AI-powered licensing system automatically clears your channel. Simply link your ID in the settings, and you'll never face a copyright strike for tracks within your active subscription tier.

Can I upgrade or downgrade anytime?

add
Yes, flexibility is key to the Tracked experience. Changes are prorated immediately, ensuring you only pay for what you use.

What are "Stems" and why do I need them?

add
Stems are the individual tracks (drums, bass, vocals, etc.) that make up a song. Pro users get these to allow for deeper editing and more professional video mixing.

Do I own the music I download?

add
You have a synchronization license for as long as you are a member. Any content published during an active subscription remains cleared forever.
